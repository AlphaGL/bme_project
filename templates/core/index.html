About Biomedical Engineering at FUTO

The modern application which involves the design, analysis and synthesis of healthy human systems or its components.

Our Mission

To provide students with a broad and balanced scientific and practical foundation to function as professional Biomedical Engineering/Technology practitioners capable of contributing meaningfully to the healthcare sector.

Our Vision

To inculcate in students a sense of enthusiasm in the profession to enhance their capacity to apply skills in biomedical technology in solving theoretical and practical healthcare problems globally.
